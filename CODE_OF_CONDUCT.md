# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AIFOXX is built in public and open to everyone. We're committed to keeping this space respectful, safe, and productive.

## ✅ Expected Behavior

- Be kind, constructive, and assume good intent
- Give actionable feedback focused on the work, not the person
- Help newcomers — everyone starts somewhere

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or abusive language
- Sharing private information without consent

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations of this policy.

## 📢 Reporting

Report conduct concerns via GitHub Discussions or a private Security Advisory thread.
